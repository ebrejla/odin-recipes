Vegetarian Gravy

Description

This flavorful gravy is made without any animal products! Gluten-free flour can even be used if desired.

Ingredients

  • ¼ C Vegetable Oil
  • ¼ C Flour
  • ½ tsp Rosemary
  • ½ tsp Thyme
  • ½ tsp Marjoram
  • ½ tsp Basil
  • 2 C Vegetable Broth
  • Soy Sauce (To Taste)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in saucepan. Add flour and cook over low heat.
  2. After a few minutes add rosemary, thyme, marjoram, and basil. Cook a few minutes longer.
  3. Add vegetable broth, a little at a time, until it reaches the desired consistency.
  4. Season with soy sauce to taste.
