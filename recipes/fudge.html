Fudge

Description

Wow your friends with this easy yet delicious fudge recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Place chocolate chips, sweetened condensed milk, and butter in a large microwaveable bowl. Microwave on medium until chips are melted, about 3-5 minutes, stirring once or twice.
  2. Pour into well-greased 8x8-inch baking dish. Refridgerate until set.
