Meatloaf

Description

This easy gluten-free meatloaf can be made with the meat of your choice. We prefer turkey or Beyond Beef to keep it lean!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. meat (turkey, plant-based substitute, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup gluten-free breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons ketchup, divided
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F. Line a standard loaf pan with aluminum foil and spray lightly with cooking oil.
  2. Place the meat, breadcrumbs, 1/4 cup ketchup, salt, onion, and egg in a large mixing bowl. Mix with hands until well combined.
  3. Place the mixture in the loaf pan, press it into the corners, and smooth the top.
  4. Spread the remaining ketchuip evenly on top of the loaf.
  5. Bake for 40 mins.
  6. Remove from oven. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing to serve.
